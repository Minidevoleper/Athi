Athidi


Unexpected guests enter a loner's house and life, unleashing a series of horrific events. Everyone becomes a suspect and a victim.

Spooked by the deserted road, Savari seeks refuge in Ravi's house. There he comes across Ravi's unfinished book, an anthology of short stories.

As Ravi narrates his stories, Savari's suspicion of Maya is confirmed during a power outage. The situation worsens when someone knocks on the door.

Savari is stunned to see who the strangers are. They threaten him to join their illegal activities. Greedy for more, they return to Ravi's house.

The unwanted guests torture Ravi for a locker room key. What happens next lands them in an inescapable situation.

The truth unfolds, and the life of a king is brought to light. As karmic paths are revealed, the sinners get their comeuppance.
